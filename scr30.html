Automated Solar Cleaning for Challenging Terrains

The CITech SCR-30 is a revolutionary solar panel cleaning robot specifically engineered for commercial and industrial rooftop solar installations. Unlike manual cleaning methods, our automated solution helps businesses reduce labor costs, minimize workplace accident risks, and optimize cleaning schedules.

By completely removing dust and dirt layers from photovoltaic cell surfaces, the SCR-30 restores light absorption efficiency, helping increase power output by 25-35%. This translates to significant ROI improvement for solar farm operators and C&I facility managers.

Key Features & Benefits

Impressive Slope Climbing

Specialized anti-slip crawler track system allows stable operation on solar panel surfaces inclined from 25° to 30°, even under wet, slippery washing conditions.

Panel Surface Protection

Aerodynamic design with ultra-lightweight materials distributes extremely low pressure (< 0.5 kg/cm²) on tempered glass surfaces, absolutely preventing micro-cracks.

Dual Cleaning Technology

Combines mechanical spiral brush (ultra-soft) with high-pressure water jets (2-8 bar) to remove stubborn dirt, bird droppings, and industrial dust.

Smart Modular Design

Quick-release assembly structure allows operators to completely disassemble/assemble the robot in just 3-5 minutes, extremely easy when moving between panel arrays.
